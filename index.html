Problem Statement

Problem Statement: Shoppers are decorating their house for the holidays and are not sure how the decorative lights they are considering buying will look like in their house. This app will allow the user to view how the lights will look in their house along with other decorations.
Who is experiencing the problem?: People that are decorating their house.
What is the problem?: Many people buy Christmas lights without first knowing how they will look in their house, potentially leading to them buying lights that dont match their other decorations.
Where does the problem present itself?: The problem comes from people buying decorative lights that dont match the decorations they already have up, making the room look tacky.
Why does it matter?: Being able to view beforehand what the lights someone is considering buying will allow them to avoid buying the wrong lights and saving hassle and money.
